#This file is a the code table for interpreting singals from the raspberry pi streamer

*************************************
1 - Turn left (Default Value)
    1xxxxx - Turn left (Defined value after first digit)
2 - Turn right (Default Value)
    2xxxxx - Turn right (Defined value after first digit)
3 - The robot is too far left (Default Value)
    3xxxxx - Adjustment
4 - The robot is too far Right (Default Value)
    4xxxxx - Adjustment
5 - The color given from the FMS is detected. STOP ROTATION
6 - Start Launch Wheel
7 - Stop Launch Wheel
*************************************
