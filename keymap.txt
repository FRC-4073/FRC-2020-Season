XBOX-360 Controller:
	Stick 1: Movement
	Stick 2: Strafe
	RB: Turn 180 Right
	LB: Turn 180 Left
	X: 100% Speed
	Y: 50% Speed
	B: 25% Speed
	A: 15% Speed
	XBOX: BeepBoop
	LT: Bring Up
	RT: Fire
	Rumble: Lined-Up

PXN Gamepad:
	Stick (Y-Axis): Manual Bring-Up
	X: Bring-Up Ball
	Y: Driver 2 Control (Non-XBOX)
	B: Driver 1 Control (XBOX)
	A: Fire Ball
	SL: Enable Camera
	SR: Disable Camera
	Home: BeepBoop

Logitech Joystick:
	Stick: Precision Control (Speed Reduced)
	Trigger: Fire
	2:  Turn 180 Right
	3:  100% Speed
	4:  Turn 90 Left
	5:  Turn 90 Right
	6:  100% Speed
	7:  15% Speed
	11: 50% Speed
	10: 25% Speed
